Our Story

Founded in 2020, CalmnClassy Dev began as a small team of passionate technologists with a shared vision: to deliver sophisticated digital solutions that combine technical excellence with elegant design.

What started as a boutique web development studio in Nairobi has grown into a full-service technology firm serving clients across East Africa and beyond. Our commitment to quality and attention to detail has earned us recognition as a trusted partner for businesses of all sizes.
